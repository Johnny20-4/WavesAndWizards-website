About the Game

Waves and Wizards is a fast paced four player FPS wave based game.

Players must work together to survive for as long as they can.

Switching staffs, purchasing upgrades and progressing through the level by collecting points.

EPIC MAGICAL TRAILER!!!

Unlock new spells and staffs as you cast your way through the hordes of monsters, and travel across the land to defeat the necromancer and end the onslaught of monsters once and for all!

Join the wizards in their fight against the creatures of the night in Waves and Wizards!
